Welcome to RCCG SOJ ChurchDB

We are pleased to have you here.

This platform has been thoughtfully designed to serve as a seamless and secure tool for collecting essential information about our church members and first-time guests.

By capturing accurate and up-to-date data, we are able to foster better communication, provide timely follow-ups, and ensure that no one is left behind in their spiritual journey.

Our goal is to build a stronger, more connected church community where everyone feels seen, supported, and valued. Whether you are registering for the first time or updating your membership details, rest assured that your information is handled with care and used solely to enhance your experience with the RCCG SOJ family.

Our Mission & Vision

  • 1) To make heaven.
  • 2) To take as many people with us.
  • 3) To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.
  • 4) To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  • 5) To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  • 6) We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ
